Sleep Study Summary

DID YOU KNOW?

  • We dream 4-6 times per night but forget 95-99% of our dreams the next morning!
  • Falling asleep takes on average 10-15 minutes. If you pass out within a few minutes, it might mean you are sleep deprived!
  • Lack of Sleep costs the United States over $411 Billion Annually, reported Fortune Magazine!
  • 35% of adults don’t get enough sleep (7 hours per day) according to the CDC!

Over the past decade, sleep disorders have become so universal in the United States that the CDC now constitutes them as a public health epidemic. This project will explore possible correlations between sleep and multiple health factors to see if there are any evident patterns across the United States. Furthermore, we were also interested in seeing how each state's median income correlated with how people slept within those states. There is much to explore about sleep and we hope to provide some exciting information to you, without making you fall asleep!

Our data was pulled directly from the CDC website. There were 5 different datasets that were used.
